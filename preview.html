Website MVP Plan For Nelson's Patina Handmade Shoe Brand

Objective

Create a sleek, professional website to showcase Nelson's premium handmade patina shoes, build brand trust, and make it easy for potential clients to contact or order.

MVP Website Structure

  1. Homepage

    • Bold tagline (e.g., “Luxury Handmade Patina Shoes”)
    • Hero photo or short video
    • Brief intro about the brand
    • Call-to-action

  2. Product Gallery

    • Showcase your top designs
    • High-quality photos with short descriptions
    • Optional Instagram/Tiktok feed for fresh updates

  3. About Page

    • Nelson's story & inspiration
    • Craftsmanship process
    • Patina technique explained

  4. Contact / Order Page

    • Simple inquiry form
    • WhatsApp/Instagram/Tiktok links
    • Option for client testimonials

Why Start with an MVP?

  • Launch quickly with a focused and elegant design
  • Collect feedback from visitors & customers
  • Expand later with features like a full shop, workshop signups, or a blog

Phase 2 Possibilities

  • Full eCommerce
  • Workshop & Challenge Signup Pages
  • Newsletter and Email Marketing
  • Shoe Customization Options
  • SEO Blog or Lookbook
  • And many more...
